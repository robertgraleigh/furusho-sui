What is a Pediatric Dentist?

Pediatric dentists have an additional 2-3 years of training to learn how to deal with the behavioral aspects of children, how to make them feel comfortable, and to make the experience pleasant in addition to clinical techniques specific to children. We are especially trained and qualified to treat patients with special needs.

What Makes  Dentistry for Kids Special?

We make an extra effort to create an exciting environment which makes a big difference, especially in the case of the very apprehensive family. By bringing your child to a pediatric dentist as early as age one year, you can ensure the timely prevention of dental disease and at the same time provide your child with an upbeat experience that can last a lifetime.
